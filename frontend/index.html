What are Urban Frenchies?

Urban Frenchies is a collection of 5,000 NFTs that come in a playful range of colors and traits. Each Urban Frenchie is a witty and faithful companion who is ready to conquer the web3 world with you. Urban Frenchies and their owners care about the planet Earth, and therefore, part of the proceeds of the project will be donated to #teamseas and #teamtrees.
